    Dirty Water

    Unity, C#, Git, Agile

    Durt

    Unity, C#, Git, Agile

    Durt is a 2D horror puzzle game. Play as Durt, a cowboy stuck in the mines of the Appalachian Mountains. These mines are filled with terrible creatures that must never be looked at. Durt must carefully shoot if he wants to make it out.
